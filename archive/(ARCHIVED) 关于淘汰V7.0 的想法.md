### **Part 2 — The Intelligent Adaptive Control Solution**（Pages 3–5）

**Page 3 — Industrial Opportunity: Adaptive Optimisation**  
**标题**：  
From Monitoring to True Adaptive Control  

**视觉布局**：  
左右强烈对比图（Current vs Needed）。

**左侧（Current Industry Approach）**：  
- In-situ monitoring (melt pool, thermal, acoustic)  
- Tells us “what happened”  
- No real-time correction  

**右侧（The Opportunity）**：  
- Adaptive optimisation in real time  
- Turns sensor data into intelligent decisions  
- Maintains stability despite changing geometry, material and thermal history  

**底部结论句**：  
**“Closing the loop with adaptive optimisation is the missing link between today’s monitoring and tomorrow’s reliable, certifiable LAM.”**

**讲解提示**：30 秒，直接呼应 Page 2 的痛点。

**Page 4 — AI-Driven Closed-Loop Control Architecture**  
**标题**：  
Our Solution: AI-Driven Closed-Loop Control  

**视觉布局**：  
一个清晰、现代的闭环示意图（比传统 PID 更智能）：
- Goal（target quality & stability）  
- ↓ AI Agent（real-time decision maker）  
- ↓ Process inputs（laser power, scan speed, etc.）  
- ↓ LAM Process  
- ↑ Multi-modal sensors（melt pool + thermal + acoustic）  
- ↺ Continuous learning & adaptation  

**内容（3 点，极简）**：  
- Moves beyond today’s monitoring-only AI  
- Makes sequential decisions under real process uncertainty  
- Optimises parameters layer-by-layer in real time  

**底部结论句**：  
**“This architecture delivers consistent quality where traditional control methods fail — directly addressing LAM’s nonlinearity, coupling and partial observability.”**

**讲解提示**：40 秒（不需要解释 RL，只说“AI agent 实时决策”即可）。

**Page 5 — Pathway to Industrial Deployment**  
**标题**：  
Real-World Validation Pathway with TWI  

**视觉布局**：  
自上而下的三步流程图（强调 TWI 在核心位置）。

**步骤**：  
1. **TWI real-world data & platforms** (first & strongest)  
   Offline + online data from industrial LAM/LMD systems  

2. **Physics-informed digital twin** (accelerator only)  
   Safe, rapid iteration of thousands of parameter combinations  

3. **Closed-loop experiments on TWI hardware** (final validation)  
   Sim-to-real transfer proven on actual industrial machines  

**底部结论句（粗体）**：  
**“80% of development effort focused on TWI experimental validation — ensuring deployability and technology readiness from day one.”**

**讲解提示**：30 秒，直接为 Part 3 的合作铺路。
