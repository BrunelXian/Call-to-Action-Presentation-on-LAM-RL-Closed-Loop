# Part 2 — The opportunity

## 5 The next step in manufacturing control

制造技术演进：

Monitoring  
↓  
Process understanding  
↓  
**Closed-loop optimisation**

核心思想：

**Use real-time data to stabilise the process.**

---

## 6 Why LAM is ideal for closed-loop optimisation

LAM 具有三个优势：

**Real-time controllable parameters**

laser power  
scan speed

**Rich sensing signals**

thermal / optical / acoustic

**Dynamic defect formation**

缺陷不是瞬间产生，而是过程演化

结论：

**LAM is one of the most suitable processes for AI-driven control.**

---

## 7 From prediction to optimisation

当前AI制造应用：

**Prediction models**

- defect prediction
- quality estimation

但工业真正需要的是：

**Process optimisation**

核心句：

**Prediction models detect problems.  
Closed-loop optimisation stabilises the process.**
